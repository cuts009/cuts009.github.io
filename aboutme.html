Biography

Cutter's first experience with college was a community college in his hometown, Amarillo College. In college, he was nominated as a tutor for the math department where he developed a love for teaching. The next year, Cutter was elected Student Body President, an experience he had not expected he would ever have. During his time in this role he made amazing friends and learned a lot about himself especially pertaining to leadership and communication. Unfortunately, he had a break in my education due to several hurdles that life threw in his direction.

For the next three years he worked a couple of jobs in food and back-end retail. He enjoyed working but his ambitions were beyond where he was going. In the Summer of 2017, Cutter and his wife just celebrated our first anniversary, they were contemplating expanding their family, Cutter wanted to finish his education, and their current lifestyle would not support these goals. Cutter began the process to join the US Air Force and enlisted early in 2018.

In the US Air Force, Cutter was trained as a Cyber Surety Technician and has worked in this job ever since. As a cyber professional, he has governed hundreds of organizations, trained even more personnel, and managed billions of dollars in equipment. In 2021, Cutter was selected as a US Space Force transfer and was one of the first enlisted members to swear into the new military force. He continues to develop his cyber security and programming expertise. He is looking forward to continuing a learn wherever he finds hiimself.
