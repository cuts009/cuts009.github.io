Breakfast Burrito Tutorial

Cutter Love
17 Oct 2021

This is an excelent tutorial done by the YouTube Channel Tasty. It is very similar to what I do in some of my meal prep. I always like to cook some green chilies in with the eggs with a little bit of chili powder, it give the burrito a nice TexMex flavor.

My favorite thing about this meal is its simplicity, as a plus it can be eaten right away or frozen to be eaten later. I like to prep a weeks worth of lunches and just warm them up for work. If I have any left over toward the end of the week they can just roll over to the next week.
